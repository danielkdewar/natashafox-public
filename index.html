Natasha Fox

Stage & Screen Actor
Contact Me

Natasha Fox is an Australian stage and screen actor based in Sydney, Australia. After completing an Advanced Diploma in Stage & Screen Acting, Natasha was busy on Brisbane's independent theatre circuit, performing exhaustively for several years before moving to New York City. After working and training in New York, Natasha relocated to Sydney, where she has most recently worked on Ruben Guthrie, Brendan Cowell's directorial debut.

Natasha is also one half of the team behind FilmADay, a film criticism website she contributes to daily.
